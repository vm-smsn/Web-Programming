What is it about?

Regular Show follows the surreal and comedic adventures of Mordecai, a laid-back blue jay, and Rigby, an energetic but lazy raccoon, who work as groundskeepers at a park. Their attempts to avoid work and slack off often lead to bizarre, supernatural, and over-the-top situations involving time travel, alternate dimensions, video game battles, and cosmic entities. They work under Benson, a hot-tempered gumball machine, and alongside Skips, an immortal yeti, Muscle Man, a green-skinned prankster, and Pops, a kind-hearted lollipop-headed man. Throughout the series, Mordecai navigates romantic troubles, and both he and Rigby mature while still maintaining their love for video games and mischief. Despite their laziness, they often rise to the occasion to save the park—and sometimes the universe—from impending doom.

Key Information

Creator: J.G. Quintel

Network: Cartoon Network

Original Run: September 6, 2010 – January 16, 2017

Genres: Comedy, Fantasy, Adventure

Main Characters

    Mordecai: A laid-back blue jay and one of the main protagonists.

    Rigby: A hyperactive raccoon and Mordecai’s best friend.

    Benson: A hot-tempered gumball machine who manages the park.

    Pops: A kind-hearted, lollipop-headed gentleman.

    Skips: An immortal yeti with deep wisdom.

    Muscle Man: A loud and obnoxious prankster.
