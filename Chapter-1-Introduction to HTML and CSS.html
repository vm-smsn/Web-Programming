Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Cinnamon rolls are sweet, soft pastries made from yeasted dough, rolled with a cinnamon-sugar filling, and baked until golden. They are typically topped with icing or glaze and are popular as a breakfast treat or dessert.

Ingredients:

For the dough:

  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup warm milk (about 110°F)
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 egg

For the filling:

  • ¼ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ⅓ cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon

For the glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Prepare the dough:
    • In a bowl, combine warm milk, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes until foamy.
    • Add melted butter, egg, salt, and flour. Mix until a soft dough forms.
    • Knead for about 5 minutes until smooth and elastic. Cover and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  2. Make the filling:
    • Mix brown sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl.
  3. Roll and Fill the Dough:
    • Roll the dough into a rectangle (about 12x9 inches).
    • Spread softened butter over the dough, then sprinkle the cinnamon-sugar mixture evenly.
  4. Shape the Rolls:
    • Roll the dough tightly from the long side to form a log.
    • Cut into 9–12 even pieces and place them in a greased baking dish.
  5. Final Rise & Bake:
    • Cover the rolls and let them rise for another 30 minutes.
    • Bake in a preheated oven at 350°F (175°C) for 20–25 minutes or until golden brown.
  6. Prepare the Glaze & Serve:
    • Mix powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.
    • Drizzle over warm cinnamon rolls and enjoy!
